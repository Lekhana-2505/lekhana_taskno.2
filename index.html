About

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was one of India's greatest scientists and the 11th President of India. He played a major role in India's missile and space programs.

Achievements

  • Developed India's missile technology
  • Known as the Missile Man of India
  • Received Bharat Ratna
  • Served as President of India (2002–2007)
